Our Story

A Legacy of Purity,
Quality & Trust

In 1984, Mr. Jaysukh Thakrar opened a small dry fruit shop on Mathuradas Road in Kandivali West, Mumbai. With a simple philosophy — serve only the best, with purity and honesty — he began a journey that would span four decades and touch thousands of families.

What started as a humble family venture has grown into one of Mumbai's most beloved dry fruit and sweets destinations. Today, Shreeji Dry Fruits operates three thriving stores across Mumbai's western suburbs, each carrying forward the same promise: Good Quality, Pure & Hygienically Made.

We supply premium dry fruits and traditional sweets not just to families, but also to caterers, wedding planners, and corporate gifting houses across the city.
